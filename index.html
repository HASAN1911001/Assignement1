Our Donate Plan

We works on the ground to protect affected people and to ensure the safety, health, nutrition, clean water, and education of children
for those affected by floods and other disasters. When a disaster strikes, your donation will make a difference. Please support us
to ensure we are ready before and when disasters happen.

Food for a family

Our primary goal is to ensure food for affected people. Your donation will help us to help those prividing food who is in need.

Shelter for a family

During flood shelter become as important as food. Your donation will help us to provide shelter for families who are homeless.

Education for a child

Your donations also help us support continued education for children during and after floods and cyclones, providing require learning facilities.

We are responsible to save our next generation

Your donations can protect children and families against waterborne diseases through the immediate supply of hygiene kits, (including soaps, toothpaste, toothbrush, comb, towels, sanitary pads, bucket, etc.), jerrycans (water containers) and water purification tablets that provide immediate relief on the ground.

Your donations can help us to support children affected by undernutrition, making sure that therapeutic milk is available at the nutrition facilities and ready for distribution.
